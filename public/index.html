My name is Serin Dunne and I currently live in Colorado Springs, Colorado. When I'm not reading a book or spending time with my friends and family, you'll find me pushing bounderies and exploring new passions. Lately, this has included singing and dancing. Back in 2022, I took a leap into the Miss Colorado USA pageant and it was a phenomenal experience. It opened my eyes to the world of modeling, introduced me to some amazing women, and challanged me to learn new things. To me, modeling is a about self-expression, storytelling and connecting with a diverse group of people; it allows me to practice my creativity and feel empowered.
